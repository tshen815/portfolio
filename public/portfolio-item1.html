Weil SolutionsInc.

SPA designed for a clients specifications

This was the first website that I created professionally from the ground up starting from a blank canvas. This single page application was designed and developed for the client who wanted to have a stronger online prescence for his business and to better inform potential future clients of his company's mission and the services provided by them.

The site was developed using React to help build rich user interfaces with Router providing responsive and fast page navigation and Hooks handling stateful logic primarily used to handle user interactions. Flexbox and grid was applied to provide a mobile-friendly and responsive design consistent throughout the site.

Firebase DB was intergrated with custom Hooks to leverage its cloud-hosted attributes for form validation and to store and sync contact inquiries from potential future clients in realtime.
